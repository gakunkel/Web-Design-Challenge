Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

There appears to be very little correlation between latitude and wind speed in either hemisphere (northern or southern). Most recorded wind speeds fell between 0 and 6 miles per hour on the day these measurements were taken. In high latitudes (both northern and souther hemispheres) there were a number of outliers with abnormally high windpseeds. Recorded wind speeds for these outliers reached as high as 13 miles per hour.
